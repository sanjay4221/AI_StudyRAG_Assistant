Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · StudyRAG AI Study Assistant
Summary: We collect your email, name, and chat history to provide the service. Your questions and document content are sent to Groq's AI servers to generate answers. You can delete your account and all your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

StudyRAG is an AI-powered study assistant that allows students to upload PDF documents and interact with the content through a chat interface. This service is operated as an educational tool.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect and store the following information:

  • Account information: Your email address, full name, and a secure hash of your password (never the plain text password)
  • Uploaded documents: PDF files you upload are stored on our server for indexing purposes
  • Chat history: Every question you ask and every answer you receive is stored to provide session history
  • Usage logs: Server logs including IP addresses and request timestamps for security and debugging

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the AI chat service — your questions and document content are processed to generate answers
  • To maintain your chat history so you can review past sessions
  • To authenticate your account securely
  • To prevent abuse through rate limiting

4. Third-Party AI Processing — Groq

Important: When you ask a question, your question text and relevant excerpts from your uploaded documents are sent to Groq's API servers located in the United States to generate an AI response. Please do not upload documents containing confidential, sensitive, or personally identifiable information.

Groq's privacy policy is available at groq.com/privacy-policy. Groq states they do not use API data to train their models.

The AI responses are generated by large language models (LLaMA, Mixtral, Gemma) and are indicated with the model name on each response bubble.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on secured servers with HTTPS encryption in transit
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we cannot recover your password
  • Each user's documents are stored in isolated spaces — other users cannot access your files
  • We implement rate limiting to protect against unauthorised access attempts

6. Your Rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and consistent with GDPR principles, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time using the "Delete Account" feature in settings
  • Portability: Request your chat history in a readable format

You can delete your account directly from the application settings, which permanently removes your email, chat history, uploaded documents, and all associated data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted from our servers within 24 hours, including your email, chat sessions, messages, and uploaded documents. Server logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security purposes.

8. Cookies and Tracking

StudyRAG does not use cookies or third-party tracking. Your JWT authentication token is stored in your browser's local storage solely to maintain your login session. We do not use advertising networks or analytics services.

9. Children's Privacy

This service is intended for users aged 16 and over, or students using it under supervision of an educator. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

All responses are generated by artificial intelligence and may contain inaccuracies, errors, or outdated information. Responses are based on the content of your uploaded documents and should not be relied upon as a substitute for professional, legal, medical, or academic advice. Always verify important information with the original source material or a qualified professional.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, please use the "Delete Account" feature within the application or contact the administrator through the platform.
